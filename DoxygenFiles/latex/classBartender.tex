\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The \mbox{\hyperlink{classBartender}{Bartender}} class is a concrete \mbox{\hyperlink{classColleague}{Colleague}} in the Mediator design pattern. 

It is responsible for creating and adding drinks to a \mbox{\hyperlink{classDrinkTray}{Drink\+Tray}}, which is then passed to the \mbox{\hyperlink{classWaiter}{Waiter}} for delivery to the customer. It receives orders from the \mbox{\hyperlink{classOrderMediator}{Order\+Mediator}} and creates the appropriate \mbox{\hyperlink{classCocktail}{Cocktail}} object.
