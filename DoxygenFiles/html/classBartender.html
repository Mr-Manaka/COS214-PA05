Detailed Description

The Bartender class is a concrete Colleague in the Mediator design pattern.

It is responsible for creating and adding drinks to a DrinkTray, which is then passed to the Waiter for delivery to the customer. It receives orders from the OrderMediator and creates the appropriate Cocktail object.
